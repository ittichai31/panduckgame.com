Based on real chaos

Inspired by Diesel, Jiji, Momo, Leaw, and Maruay.

The game is built around the feeling of living near cats: affectionate, unpredictable, funny, independent, and sometimes very confident about blocking exactly the thing you were using.

What to expect

Built to quietly become part of your routine.

Play with toys: balls, puzzle toys, robot toys, and wand toys for cats to chase, pounce, or completely ignore.

Shape the room: arrange furniture, shelves, beds, and playful objects into a tiny desktop cat home.

Keep growing: Panduck plans to keep adding new toys, furniture, and cat breeds if the game gains enough traction.
